Investment Criteria

How We Evaluate

Every deal is scored on a 100-point quantitative model. Basis discount to market is the single most important factor.

01

Below Replacement Cost

We buy at a meaningful discount to what it would cost to build new. This is our margin of safety and the highest-weighted scoring factor.

02

Secondary MSAs

Markets with 300K–1.5M population where institutional buyers haven't compressed cap rates. Texas border towns, Carolinas, Louisville, Gainesville, and the Midwest.

03

$1M – $5M Deals

Small enough to avoid institutional competition, large enough to generate meaningful returns on 3–5 year hold periods.

04

Value-Add Potential

Capital improvements, lease-up, operational efficiency, or repositioning — we target properties where active management creates value.

The Principal

Nate Wellek

Nate is the founder and sole principal of Duffield Holdings, where he manages all aspects of deal sourcing, underwriting, investor relations, and asset management across the firm's industrial real estate portfolio.

He also serves as VP of Finance & Operations at Candyroot Lodge Holdings, overseeing financial modeling, capital raises, HR, and daily operations for a 1,250-acre multi-course golf destination under development in Jefferson, South Carolina.

Prior to launching Duffield, Nate led financial underwriting for multi-billion-dollar industrial and mixed-use redevelopment projects at Hilco Redevelopment Partners (now HRP Group), including The Bellwether District in Philadelphia and HRP Alexandria. He previously worked as an Asset Analyst at The Scion Group, a leading student housing owner-operator.
